mAKEAThOn

Inter College Hackethon

India, a nation projected to drive the global production in span of 10 years, is facing many issues and computer science and IOT serves as the o to solution for many of these issues. Our hackathon looks to harness the power of amazing developers and the world of open source to develop state of the art to few problems. Our hackathon aligns with the mission and vision of Make in india and prime minister's Digital India Campaign. The hackers will be required to present a viable prototype of the solution they are building.
